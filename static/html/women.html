Women's Ministry

Women Called to succeed is a group of women who were born in this ministry, and whose call to serve is genuine, through the Holy Spirit. These women are a very important part of the ministry, they love, have passion , and vision to prosper. They know what they want to achieve, they are defined as strong women and give love for woman who joins our ministry. Women called to succeed have goals and purposes: raising women , showing their value, and route them to have a closer relationship with God. Every woman is determined to fulfill each purpose for the glory of God.

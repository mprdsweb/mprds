About Us

Ministerios Profetico Roca de Salvacion was founded on Magnolia Street the city of Fullerton on the the 20th of November 2007. It was led by a man with passion in the belief of God. It was his loyalty and commitment to the gospel that started from his own living room, feeling a sudden desire to fulfill a dream that God had placed in his heart just how God has used many men before. In the book of Genesis, God had called a leader to rise, a champion, with the sole purpose to lead a nation from the hands of Egypt to a new beginning. For that reason Roca de Salvacion has the same desire to restore a community back to the laws of God. It all starts in Fullerton, spreading the gospel in correctional facilities, our local neighborhood, parks, etc. Roca de Salvacion was built from the ground up but with the grace of God it has gained wisdom and the desire for a higher reach. Starting with only a simple podium and a couple chairs and a small group that consisted of only his wife and kids and another member. Having no experience, no musical instruments, he decided to act and purchase a small cd player, it was the only method of worship, singing along to the music. Soon after he realized that his children needed to learn a musical instrument to to have better worship atmosphere. It was so that they began to prepare themselves for the glory of God. As months passed the ministry began to grow before his eyes, he knocked walls down in order to extend. After a year the ministry had grown in number and no longer fit in the house it was time to relocate.

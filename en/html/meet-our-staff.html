Francisco Hernandez

Head Pastor

My name is Francisco Alfredo Hernandez was born on 1969 San Salvador in the department of Morazán. My dream since childhood was to work for the community by helping children, because there was much need for poverty in my country. Migrate to the United States in 1998, when I enter this country everything changed. I faced many situations not know the place, but my dreams were deep inside my heart and not the way how to proceed. My dreams in 1993 met Mr. At that time he was already married to my wife, Blanca Lidia Vicente Hernandez and already had a child and could not find how to impact or help the community until one day someone told me about Jesucristo. El Lord is the all he could help me to bring a word of encouragement to others. That's how I start to work in a church, the Christian doctrine fundamentally helped me in my life, and for me is very special. I know there are people in need of a change in life and only God can give, because outside of it there is no peace. I am now the pastor of the church, where he worked with a team to help the community and help the government reduce violence resulting studies that touch the human heart. If anyone is interested in knowing more about this ministry feel free to contact us

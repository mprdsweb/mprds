Worship

Juventud en Avivamiento (JEA) was led by a passionate believer in God. Juventud en Avivamiento began with little to no knowledge pertaining to music. It was through the holy spirit that their knowledge in music grew, this was the start of Juventud en Avivamiento. Pastor Hernandez was able to rely on each member of the group. Our first project debuted “Quiero Vivir” paid homage to the holy trinity. Juventud en Avivamiento is a group of young teens who have devoted their lives, their time, and their service to the lord. God’s plans are always perfect, each member was an import piece to the ministry. It was Pastor Hernandez who was author and composer of the Album “Quiero Vivir” as we continue to prepare more worshipers.

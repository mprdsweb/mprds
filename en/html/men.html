Men's Ministry

As leaders in the home, church, and community; it is of great importance to build men who are actively involved to be men of God and be able to be an example for family. There is a need for leaders within our church and community. As believers in Christ, we need to equip people of our body and the future generation to come.

The men in our ministry accomplish this through many different activities held. Our knights are working on missions projects to help poor families in Honduras to contribute a grain of sand in this world. We have to make a radical change in men so they can be the best in the business, their family, and their home.
